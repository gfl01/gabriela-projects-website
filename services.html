What We Offer

Design to Build, Under One Roof

With both design and construction under one roof, and experience navigating NYC's unique challenges, we deliver spaces that are functional, beautiful, and fully compliant.

1. Design & Material Selection

We help bring your vision to life with thoughtful design and expert guidance. Our in-house designer works closely with you to create functional, beautiful spaces that reflect your style and needs.

What we offer:

  • Millwork & Custom Furniture Design – Tailored pieces that fit your space perfectly
  • 3D Renderings & Visualizations – See your project before it's built
  • Space Planning & Layouts – Optimize flow, function, and aesthetics
  • Material & Finish Selection – Guidance on wood, metals, stone, and other finishes

2. Approvals & Project Coordination

Navigating renovations in New York City can be complex. With years of experience building across the boroughs, we handle the critical behind-the-scenes work so you don't have to.

We provide:

  • Assistance with building approvals and permits
  • Coordination with specialized contractors and engineers
  • Ensuring compliance with NYC building codes and regulations

This step ensures your project is fully ready for construction, avoiding delays and surprises.

3. Construction & Fabrication

From concept to completion, we bring your designs to life with precision craftsmanship and reliable execution.

What we offer:

  • Renovations & Remodels
  • Custom Millwork Fabrication & Installation
  • Carpentry & Built-Ins
  • Kitchen & Bathroom Projects
  • End-to-End Project Management
