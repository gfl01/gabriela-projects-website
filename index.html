Who We Are

Women Owned.
Women Led.

Gabriela Flores, the heart and soul behind every project, brings warmth, creativity, and a sharp eye for detail. Originally from Peru and now based in New York City, she has spent many years working across the boroughs, navigating a wide range of projects and challenges that have shaped her into a skilled and adaptable professional.

Her commitment to quality craftsmanship, thoughtful design, and reliable execution has earned the trust of clients, architects, and colleagues, resulting in lasting relationships, referrals, and repeat business.
