How It Works

Our Process

A Clear Path from Vision to Reality

Renovating in New York City comes with unique challenges — from building approvals and co-op boards to tight timelines and complex logistics. Our structured, five-phase process is designed to guide you through every step with clarity and confidence, so you can focus on the exciting part: watching your space transform.

01

Phase One

Discovery & Consultation

Every project begins with a conversation. We visit your space, listen to your goals, and discuss your vision, lifestyle, and budget. Whether you're reimagining a pre-war apartment on the Upper East Side or modernizing a Brooklyn brownstone, this initial meeting sets the foundation for everything that follows.

In-Home Walkthrough

We assess your space in person — evaluating the layout, existing conditions, and any structural considerations specific to your building.

Scope & Budget Alignment

We define a realistic scope of work and provide a preliminary estimate, so you know what to expect before we begin.

Detailed Proposal

You receive a comprehensive proposal outlining the project phases, estimated timeline, and investment breakdown.

02

Phase Two

Design & Material Selection

With design and construction under one roof, our in-house designer collaborates closely with you to develop layouts, finishes, and details that reflect your taste and work within your budget. No surprises, no guesswork.

Space Planning & Layouts

We optimize the flow and function of your space, exploring multiple layout options to find the one that works best for how you live.

3D Renderings

See your space before a single wall is touched. Our visualizations let you experience the design and make confident decisions early on.

Material & Finish Guidance

From countertops and cabinetry to tile and hardware, we guide you through every selection — balancing aesthetics, durability, and budget.

03

Phase Three

Approvals & Pre-Construction

NYC renovations require navigating a layer of approvals that most cities don't. We handle this complexity so you don't have to — from DOB filings and permits to co-op and condo board packages.

Permits & Filings

We coordinate with architects and expediters to secure all required permits and Department of Buildings approvals before construction begins.

Board & Building Approvals

For co-ops and condos, we prepare the alteration agreement packages, insurance certificates, and construction schedules your building requires.

Subcontractor Coordination

Licensed plumbers, electricians, and other specialized trades are lined up and scheduled — ensuring the right people are ready at the right time.

04

Phase Four

Construction & Fabrication

This is where your vision comes to life. Our dedicated team manages every aspect of construction, from demolition through final finishes, keeping you informed at every milestone.

Dedicated Project Management

A single point of contact oversees your project day-to-day — coordinating trades, managing the schedule, and resolving issues before they become problems.

Custom Millwork & Carpentry

Built-ins, custom cabinetry, and bespoke furniture pieces are fabricated and installed by our in-house team with precision craftsmanship.

Regular Updates

You'll receive progress photos and updates throughout construction, so you always know exactly where your project stands — even if you're not on-site.

05

Phase Five

Final Walkthrough & Handover

Before we hand you the keys to your newly transformed space, we make sure every detail meets our standards — and yours.

Quality Inspection

We conduct a thorough walkthrough together, reviewing every element of the finished space to ensure it matches the approved design and meets our quality standards.

Punch List & Touch-Ups

Any final adjustments are documented and addressed promptly, so your space is move-in ready with nothing left undone.

Post-Project Support

Our relationship doesn't end at handover. We stand behind our work and are here for any questions or needs that come up after you've settled in.

Why It Matters

Built for New York City

Our process was built specifically for the realities of renovating in one of the most complex construction environments in the country, so nothing catches you off guard.
